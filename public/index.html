Why Blockchain Voting?

Secure

Cryptographic security ensures votes cannot be tampered with or altered after casting.

Transparent

All votes are recorded on the blockchain, providing a verifiable audit trail.

Private

Voter anonymity is maintained while ensuring each voter can only vote once.
